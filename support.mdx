## When you write in

The faster we can reproduce the issue, the faster we can fix it. Where it makes sense, please include:

- **What you were trying to do** — in one sentence
- **What you saw instead** — a screenshot is ideal
- **Where in Reeva** — agent name, integration name, or page URL
- **When it happened** — approximate time, with your timezone
- **Who's affected** — just you, your team, or all your users

## Response times

- **Standard support** — we respond within one business day, Monday through Friday
- **Outage or production-blocking issue** — please mark the email subject with `URGENT` and include the words "production blocking" in the body

## Status and incidents

If multiple things look broken at once and you suspect a Reeva-side incident, email support and we'll confirm. We'll also reach out proactively to your account contacts if there's a wider issue.

## Product version

This documentation reflects **Reeva v0.1**.

If you're on a different version, some screens or capabilities may differ slightly. Email [support@reeva.ai](mailto:support@reeva.ai) if you need version-specific guidance.

## Feedback

We read every piece of feedback. If something is confusing, missing, or just feels rough, please tell us — even small notes are useful.
